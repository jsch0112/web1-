JK란 누구인가?

Jeon Jung-kook, better known mononymously as Jungkook, is a South Korean singer, songwriter and record producer. He is a member and vocalist of the South Korean boy band BTS.

His family consists of his parents and an elder brother. He attended Baekyang Elementary and Middle School in Busan. When he became a trainee, he transferred to Singu Middle School in Seoul.Jungkook initially had dreams of becoming a badminton player when he was young, but after seeing G-Dragon perform "Heartbreaker" on television, it influenced him to want to become a singer.
